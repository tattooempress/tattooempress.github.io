Tattoo Prices at the 'Imperatrix' Studio

The price of a tattoo depends on the complexity of the design, style, and size. Sometimes, multiple sessions are needed to create the perfect tattoo.

Preparing for the Consultation

Before calling or writing, we recommend that you choose a tattoo. You can provide us with a sketch of the future tattoo or prepare a detailed description of what it should look like. If you're unsure about your choice and need assistance, feel free to call us; we are here to help!

Approximate Price List for Creating a Tattoo

Complexity Price (EUR)
Low (small, simple tattoos) 100 - 150
Medium (medium size and complexity) 150 - 300
High (large, complex tattoos) 300 and above
