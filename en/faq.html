Frequently Asked Questions

We understand that you may have questions about the tattooing process, care, and other aspects. Below, we've compiled frequently asked questions from our clients along with their answers.

Common Questions

1. How do I choose a tattoo?

Before choosing a tattoo, think about what it will mean to you. Select a sketch or prepare a description, and our artists will help you with your choice.

2. How does the tattooing process work?

The process starts with a consultation where we discuss your ideas and preferences. Then we create a sketch and begin the tattoo, adhering to all safety measures.

3. How long does it take to get a tattoo?

The time depends on the complexity and size of the tattoo. Typically, it takes from one hour to several sessions.

4. How do I care for my tattoo after getting it?

Caring for a tattoo involves regular cleaning and moisturizing of the skin. Avoid direct sunlight and water bodies for the first few weeks.

5. Does getting a tattoo hurt?

Discomfort varies depending on the location of the tattoo and individual sensitivity. We strive to make the process as comfortable as possible.

6. How do I choose the size and location for my tattoo?

The choice of size and location depends on your preferences and the tattoo idea. We can offer recommendations based on your sketch.

7. What materials are used for tattoos?

We use only high-quality, certified inks and sterile tools to ensure the safety of our clients.

8. Can I change the sketch before the session?

Yes, you can make changes to the sketch right up until the session starts. We want you to be completely satisfied with the result.

9. Are there age restrictions for getting a tattoo?

You must be at least 18 years old to get a tattoo. If you are younger, parental consent is required.

10. How can I book a session?

You can book a session through our website or by contacting us by phone. We are always ready to help you with your choice and answer your questions.

We Look Forward to Seeing You!

If you have additional questions, feel free to reach out to us. We are always happy to help!
